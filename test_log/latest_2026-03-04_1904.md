## 7. Hypothesis Status (MECE)
### 7.1 Validated / strongly supported
1. Base64-heavy/large payload paths are expensive.
2. Chatty transfer pattern is slower than batched pattern.
3. Main-thread style decode/processing creates stall risk.
4. Missing multipart dependency blocks endpoint (`python-multipart`).
5. Backend normalization mitigates image-input invalid failures on tested set.

### 7.2 Partially validated
1. Norm-on improves reliability tail vs norm-off (early signal, low n).
2. Cold-start contributes to outlier behavior (supported by observed first-request spikes + Render free-tier behavior docs).

### 7.3 Not yet fully validated
1. High-confidence p99 comparison between norm-on and norm-off (insufficient sample).
2. Full concurrency saturation curve under controlled windows.
3. Failover-event latency impact under sustained quota pressure.
